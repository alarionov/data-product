What is MPG?
========================================================

MPG (Miles per gallon) is a measure of the average distance traveled per gallon of gasoline.

### Why MPG is important?
- Saves You Money
- Reduces Climate Change
- Reduces Oil Dependence Costs
- Increases Energy Sustainability
